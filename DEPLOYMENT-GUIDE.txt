## üéØ RECOMMENDED: GitHub Pages (FREE & EASIEST)

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

**1. Create GitHub Account**
- Go to https://github.com/signup
- Sign up with your email
- Verify your account

**2. Create New Repository**
- Click the "+" button (top right) ‚Üí "New repository"
- Repository name: `5-pillar-weight-loss`
- Description: "Weight loss affiliate website"
- Select "Public"
- Check "Add a README file"
- Click "Create repository"

**3. Upload Website Files**
- Click "Add file" ‚Üí "Upload files"
- Drag and drop ALL files from the ZIP folder
- OR click "choose your files" and select all
- Wait for upload to complete
- Click "Commit changes"

**4. Enable GitHub Pages**
- Go to "Settings" tab (top of page)
- Scroll down to "Pages" section (left sidebar)
- Under "Source", select "Deploy from a branch"
- Select "main" branch
- Click "Save"

**5. Your Site is Live!**
- Wait 2-5 minutes
- Your URL: `https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/5-pillar-weight-loss/`
- Share this link with customers!
