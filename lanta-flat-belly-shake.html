How To Use

1

Measure

Add one scoop (included) of Lanta Flat Belly Shake powder to your shaker bottle or blender.

2

Mix

Add 8-10 oz of water, unsweetened almond milk, or your preferred beverage. Shake or blend until smooth.

3

Enjoy

Consume immediately. Best used as a breakfast replacement or post-workout recovery drink within 30 minutes of exercise.

4

Storage

Store powder in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate mixed shake if not consumed immediately and use within 24 hours.
